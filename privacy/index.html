Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 27, 2026

Introduction

Maxine is developed and published by donnywals.com BV, a company registered in the Netherlands. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Maxine app.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. All workout data, exercise logs, goals, and preferences you enter in Maxine are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this information, and it is never transmitted to our servers.

Analytics

Maxine does not use any third-party analytics services. The only analytics data that may be collected is through Apple's built-in App Analytics, which Apple provides to developers in aggregated and anonymized form. This data helps us understand general usage trends but does not identify individual users. You can opt out of sharing this data with developers in your device's Settings under Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

In-App Purchases

Maxine offers in-app purchases through Apple's App Store, processed via RevenueCat. When you make a purchase:

  • Apple acts as the merchant of record and processes your payment.
  • RevenueCat manages purchase validation and entitlements on our behalf.
  • We do not receive or store your payment details, credit card information, or billing address.
  • Purchase receipts are validated anonymously to unlock features in the app.

For information on how Apple handles your payment data, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

Your workout data is stored exclusively on your device. If you use iCloud backup, your app data may be included in your device backup according to your iCloud settings. We have no access to your iCloud data. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and any backups.

Children's Privacy

Maxine is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of us holding children's information.

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), EU residents have certain rights regarding personal data. Since Maxine does not collect or process any personal data, these rights do not apply in practice. However, if you have any questions or concerns, you may contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us
