Most training mistakes are not dramatic. They are small habits repeated for months. The good news is that small fixes create fast improvements.

Mistake one: changing programs constantly. Fix: pick one solid plan and run it long enough to evaluate.

Mistake two: no tracking. Fix: log sets, reps, load, and effort. If you can measure it, you can improve it.

Mistake three: too much intensity, not enough recovery. Fix: train hard, but keep most sets controlled and schedule deloads when needed.

Mistake four: chasing soreness instead of progression. Fix: judge sessions by performance trends, not pain levels.

Mistake five: overcomplicating nutrition. Fix: protein target, calorie control, hydration, and basic food quality first.

If you clean up these basics, your results will look very different in a few months.
